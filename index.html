Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Thank you for using Alfabetica (the "App"). This policy explains how we handle data when you use the Android App.

Controller

The controller for data related to Alfabetica is the developer of Alfabetica ("Controller"). This policy applies to the Android App.

Data We Collect

When you use the Android App, the App stores preferences locally on your device, such as categories, selected letters, sound, and timer duration. This storage happens on the device using Android mechanisms such as preferences DataStore.

The App uses Firebase Analytics for usage and performance analytics. According to the data disclosure submitted to Google Play, the App may collect:

  • App info and performance: crash logs, diagnostics, and other app performance data, for analytics purposes.
  • Device or other IDs: device or other IDs, for analytics purposes.

The App does not show ads at this time. Advertising ID collection and ad personalization signals have been disabled in the App.

Purposes and Legal Bases

  • Operate and maintain the App, including functionality and security. Legal basis: contract performance and legitimate interest.
  • User preferences in the App, such as letters, sound, and timer, to personalize the experience. Legal basis: contract performance.
  • Analytics for usage, crashes, diagnostics, performance, and aggregated metrics to understand how the App is used and improve quality. Legal basis: legitimate interest or consent, where required by applicable law.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. According to the data disclosure submitted to Google Play, the App does not share user data with other companies or organizations.

Services such as Firebase Analytics may process data as service providers to operate the App's analytics features, according to their own policies and applicable terms.

Ads

The App does not show ads at this time and does not use ads SDKs.

Retention

  • App: preferences remain stored locally until you delete them in the App settings or uninstall the App.
  • Analytics: analytics data is retained according to the applicable Firebase/Google Analytics settings and policies.

Your Rights

Under applicable law, including the Brazilian LGPD and, where applicable, European data protection rules, you may request access, correction, deletion, portability, information about processing and sharing, objection, or withdrawal of consent when consent is the legal basis used.

Children and Teenagers

Alfabetica does not knowingly collect personal data from children or teenagers. If you are responsible for a child and believe data was improperly provided, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and transferred over a secure connection. Still, no service is 100% secure; we recommend keeping your device and the App updated.

International Transfers

Services such as Firebase/Google Analytics may process data on infrastructure located outside your country. Where applicable, we seek to comply with legal requirements for international transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the App, SDKs used, or applicable law. The current version will always be the most recent version indicated at the top of this page.
